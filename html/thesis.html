LaTeX Math Equation Demo

Here are some examples of math equations in LaTeX:

Inline Math

The quadratic formula is written as $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$.

Displayed Equations

The same formula in a displayed equation: $$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$

Aligned Equations

Solving a system of linear equations: $$\begin{aligned} 2x + 3y &= 5 \\ 4x - y &= 7 \end{aligned}$$

Matrices

A 3x3 identity matrix: $$I_3 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$$

Summations and Integrals

A summation example: $$S = \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{1}{n^2}$$

An integral example: $$\int_a^b x^2 \, dx = \frac{b^3}{3} - \frac{a^3}{3}$$

Greek Letters and Symbols

Using Greek letters and mathematical symbols: $$\alpha + \beta = \gamma, \quad \nabla \cdot \mathbf{F} = \frac{\partial F_x}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial F_y}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial F_z}{\partial z}$$
